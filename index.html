Partners

Monax Labs/Aspen NFT: Monax is an institutional leader in the innovation and the implementation of the IP/legal framework within the NFT space. Immediate partnership encompasses: minting, legal/terms & conditions, marketing and general advisory.
Non-Fungible Films: The first Web3 native studio helping bring navitve Web3 projects to Hollywood. They have a large, growing community and strong relationshops with projects like BAYC/MAYC, Cool Cats and Pudgy Penguins. They are also developing projects with major studios and videogame IP holders. We plan on collaborating on a variety of Web3 projects.
LBI: LBI Entertainment (management firm to talent including Jamie Foxx and Jonah Hill among others) is NL’s producing partner in the Hollywood ecosystem. NL is actively developing and packaging film and tv with LBI. LBI also represents Bobby Hundreds (well-known NFT artist/fashion designer), who is a potential merchandise partner for NL NFT. LBI is working in partnership with NL NFT to prodcure talent partners and content for the Poison Pill effort.
